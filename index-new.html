CEO/Founder

Ed has over twenty‐seven years of experience leading progressive and innovative teams of security professionals. He was also the Director of the National Transportation Security Operations Center for the U.S. Department of Homeland Security, where he was responsible for building and operating one of the largest command centers in the world. Ed holds numerous patents for innovative security devices that are driving fundamental changes within the security industry. He is a much sought after technical advisor to other tech startups in the industry.
