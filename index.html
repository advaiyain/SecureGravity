    Our Team

    CEO/Founder

    Ed Bacco

    Ed has over twenty‐seven years of experience leading progressive and innovative teams of security professionals. This experience includes seven years at Amazon where he was the Head of Corporate Security and responsible for the development and deployment of global physical security systems and devices at hundreds of sites located in thirty‐nine different countries.

    He was also the Director of the National Transportation Security Operations Center for the U.S. Department of Homeland Security, where he was responsible for building and operating one of the largest command centers in the world. The center coordinated the security at over 440 international airports, seaports, pipelines, rail and public transportation nodes. Read More

    Ed holds numerous patents for innovative security devices that are driving fundamental changes within the security industry. He is a much sought after technical advisor to other tech startups in the industry.

    President and COO

    Domenic Prinzivalli

    Domenic has over twenty years of executive experience operating businesses in the security industry. This included five years as Amazon.com’s Head of Global Security where he set the vision for a program known for consistently developing innovative solutions that secured their employees, physical assets, and information at their offices, fulfillment centers and data centers across the globe. He takes pride in developing and motivating his employees, and creating a culture that thrives on finding unique and creative ways to resolve complex issues. Read More

    Prior to that he was the COO at a division of G4S, the world’s leading security provider, that revolutionized the electronic monitoring industry by designing and utilizing the first web based offender tracking and case management system as well as manufacturing GPS, cellular, and radio frequency transmitters (one fourth the size of competitors). This equipment tracked offenders’ locations and alerted their probation and/or parole officer of violations.

    Domenic earned a Master of Business Administration from the University of Southern California and a Bachelor of Business Administration from the University of Notre Dame where he also played varsity football and wrestled.


    Director of Product Management

    Anthony Torrero Collins

    Anthony has designed, built and delivered innovative and effective software applications and systems for some of the world’s leading technical and engineering organizations. His experience includes technical work across the business landscape including security, manufacturing, retail, supply chain, e-commerce and software application. He draws on his diverse experience as a successful technical project manager, software developer, technical writer, web programmer, application consultant and electronics technician to provide creative solutions to every problem encountered in bringing products and programs to completion.

    Anthony attended the University of Washington where he earned a Bachelor of Arts in English, and a Masters Degree in Information Science. Rounding out his education is a Certificate in Advanced Project Management from the Stanford University Professional Development Center.


    Board Member

    Phil Aronson

    Phil has more than 25 years of physical security solutions experience including access control, video and door hardware. Phil is best known for his singular vision and passion for redefining the industry’s approach to the client. He is the President of ASG were he has worked since 1983. Prior to ASG, he worked in the computer industry for many years.

    He created The ASG Path to Value to organize ASG’s core processes and expertise around a client’s business and security needs. He also authored The ASG Way, a manifesto for his company in attracting the best in the industry, creating a culture of accountability and excellence, and providing them a vision for what a great company should be. This has made ASG one of the premier risk and security integrators in the world. Under his leadership, ASG’s Global Accounts presence has grown to include clients in 49 states, and 27 countries.
